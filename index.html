Frequently Asked Questions

I offer a range of design services including branding, UI/UX design, illustration, web design, and visual storytelling. Each project is tailored to meet specific client needs.

Additionally, I also offer illustration services such as drawing requests, icons (for YouTube, Instagram, Twitch, etc.), stickers, and many more.

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Smaller projects may take 2-4 weeks, while larger branding or web design projects can take 6-12 weeks.

Same works for illustration projects, and the amount of assets requested. If it is just a single illustration, it may take 4-10 hours depending on complexity. More illustrations can take up to days.

My process includes: discovery and research, concept development, design iterations, client feedback rounds, and final delivery with all necessary files and documentation.

The process is quite similar for illustrations, since I want to understand my client's needs before I start sketching. I also like to send the sketch for analysis before I start the lineart.
