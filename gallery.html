Gallery

This is where it all started. I grew up watching anime with my parents, sketching characters long before I understood anything about art. By thirteen, I began teaching myself, and over the years I slowly shaped my own style and original characters. Now, at nineteen, drawing remains one of the things I love most.

These works showcase some of my favorite creations from 2024 to today. There isn’t a strict theme—I often draw whatever feels right in the moment. That spontaneity is part of what makes these pieces meaningful to me. I hope you enjoy exploring them.

While I still love traditional sketching, there came a point when I felt I could grow beyond paper. I started teaching myself digital art, and it opened up an entirely new way of illustrating. More color, more expression, and more room to explore different concepts.

These are the digital pieces I’ve been working on. Like my paper drawings, there’s no strict theme; many are inspired by lyrics from songs I love, and some feature characters from other media that I admire. Maybe you’ll recognize a few.
